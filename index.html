HOW IT PRINTS

01

Wireframe (Yellow)

Lay down the foundational ideas. Yellow acts as the subtle base layer. Fast, loose, and easily overwritten by the heavier inks.

02

Structure (Pink)

Build the components. The fluorescent pink layer defines the grid, the typography hierarchy, and the interactive states.

03

Details (Cyan)

The intricate details. Icons, micro-copy, and borders. Cyan cuts through the warm colors to provide sharp contrast.

04

Registration (Black)

The final pass. Perfectly misaligned text outlines the final deliverable. Ready to ship, beautifully flawed.
